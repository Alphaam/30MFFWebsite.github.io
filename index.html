Explore the award winning quarantine film "things in Quarantine"

THE GENIUS

How did it all begin? The award winning short film, Things in Quarantine, is four earnest creators' vision to showcase what life in quarantine is truly like. The characters are the people, their perspectives are THE perspectives and the result is this masterpiece.

MEET THE CAST

Banana

The Banana represents the overtly exaggerated optimism that some entered into quarantine with. He is a symbol of perserverence of positivity regardless of how hard times get.

Tea Bag

She is all that we all are on our worst day. Hoisted over a boiling cup of water she represents the constant trial our patience gets put under and the sporadic cracking when it all gets just too much.

Apple

The Apple is when quarantine finally feels like a day off. No awkwardly adjusting to life in the time of a pandemic just Netflix and peace and, ofcourse, the occasional panic attack.

Spoon

What about when you're having a a good day? You were able to see your friends, your sleep felt restful, SZA finally released an album? On this day of quarantine, we are the Spoon.
